# Marketing ROI & Budget Reallocation Analysis

## Project Overview

This capstone project analyzes marketing campaign performance and provides data-driven recommendations for budget reallocation to maximize Return on Investment (ROI). The objective is to identify high-performing campaigns, optimize marketing spend, and support strategic business decision-making through analytics.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often invest significant amounts in marketing campaigns without having complete visibility into campaign effectiveness and profitability. Inefficient budget allocation can lead to wasted spend, lower ROI, and missed revenue opportunities.

This project addresses these challenges by analyzing campaign performance data and recommending optimized budget allocation strategies based on measurable business outcomes.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze marketing campaign performance across channels.
- Measure Return on Investment (ROI).
- Identify high-performing and underperforming campaigns.
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness using business KPIs.
- Recommend budget reallocation strategies to improve profitability.
- Support data-driven decision-making through analytics.

## Key Analysis Performed

### Campaign Performance Analysis
- Evaluated revenue generated by each campaign.
- Compared campaign spend against revenue generated.
- Identified high-performing marketing channels.

### ROI Analysis
- Calculated campaign-level ROI.
- Compared profitability across campaigns.
- Identified opportunities for budget optimization.

### Budget Reallocation Assessment
- Evaluated underperforming campaigns.
- Recommended strategic reallocation of marketing spend.
- Prioritized investment toward high-return campaigns.

---

## Key Insights

- High-performing campaigns generated significantly greater ROI than other channels.
- Certain campaigns consumed a large portion of marketing spend while contributing limited revenue.
- Budget reallocation opportunities were identified that could improve overall marketing effectiveness.
- Data-driven decision-making can significantly improve marketing efficiency and profitability.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Increase investment in campaigns generating the highest ROI.
2. Reduce spend on low-performing campaigns.
3. Implement periodic campaign performance reviews.
4. Use KPI-driven marketing allocation strategies.
5. Monitor campaign performance through executive dashboards.
6. Establish data-driven budget governance processes.
